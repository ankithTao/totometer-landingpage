Meet Todometer—the no-BS to-do list that shows your progress as a ring. Add a task, slide the gauge, and watch momentum build. Attach links or photos, set a due date, and let gentle reminders keep you on track. When it’s time to concentrate, start the built-in focus timer. It’s simple, fast, and private—on iPhone, iPad, and visionOS.

What you’ll love
• A progress ring for every task
• One-tap capture with notes, links, and photos
• Due-date reminders you control
• Clear analytics to see what matters today
• A focus timer with end-of-session alerts
• Your data stays on your device—no accounts, no ads

Available on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and visionOS
